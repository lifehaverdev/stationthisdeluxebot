About NOEMA

NOEMA is an independent AI lab that ships uncensored, fully-custom models. Everything we build is open-source and crypto-native.

What You Get

All code and weights are public. The essentials are below.

  • Usage-based pricing. Buy the points you need; no subscriptions, no lock-ins.
  • Creator stack included. NFT collection generator, model training, and dataset tools built-in.
  • Incentive engine. Contribute datasets or custom workflows and earn automatic rewards.
  • Multi-Platform. Web Canvas, Telegram bot, REST & WebSocket APIs; Discord coming soon.
  • Cypherpunk-aligned. Open repos, transparent weights, zero censorship filters.

Standardly purchased unused points can be withdrawn at any time—gas fees only.

Open Source, Always.

Every dataset, weight file, and line of code is public. Audit it, fork it, improve it.

Built by Builders.

The lab is owned and steered by the people who ship workflows and datasets—not investors or gatekeepers.

Values Over Hype.

No paywalls, no SaaS handcuffs. We’re here for the long-term stack, not the short-term trend cycle.

The full architecture, smart-contract code, and model specs are one click away. Explore, audit, and decide if it fits your build.
